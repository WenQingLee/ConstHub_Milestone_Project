About ConstHub

ConstHub is one of Singapore's leading solutions provider for construction professionals. Established since 2015,

Our integrated platform will digitise your site processes and provide access to information at any time from anywhere with real-time syncing. Coupled with analytics, it provides insights on operations from data captured to streamline projects


We help companies in the building and construction industry drive their digital transformation through cutting-edge technology. With Novade’s enterprise platform, site processes are made digital with mobile applications. The data captured in the field provides insights to streamline operations and improve quality, safety and productivity. Novade is a global organization operating across Europe and Asia. Our platform is adopted by some of the largest general contractors and real estate companies in the world.

Technology is disrupting the industry.

Compared to other industries, building and construction has a lower tech adoption rate. Yet, a combination of technologies – BIM, IOT, AI and mobile technology – is on the verge of disrupting the entire industry. Why now? Because these technologies are now relevant in the field. When all site processes are digitized, each team member can be affordably equipped to access information in real time. Teams can then connect and gather data from buildings and equipment, analyse millions of records and more, all on their mobile devices.
